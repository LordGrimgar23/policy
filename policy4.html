Privacy Policy

Navine Homework Helper

This Privacy Policy explains how Navine Homework Helper handles user data. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how this application works.

Overview

Navine Homework Helper is an educational tool designed to assist students with homework, studying, learning concepts, solving academic problems, and improving understanding across various subjects. The app is intended to provide educational support and learning assistance.

Data Collection

Navine Homework Helper does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information for advertising purposes.

  • No personal information is sold to third parties
  • No student data is shared for marketing purposes
  • No browsing activity outside the app is tracked
  • No unnecessary personal information is collected

Educational Content

Any homework questions, assignments, notes, or educational content entered into the application are used solely to provide learning assistance and educational support. Such content is not used for advertising or profiling.

Permissions

Any permissions requested by Navine Homework Helper are used exclusively to enable app functionality, such as accessing uploaded homework images, documents, or educational materials when required by the user. Permissions are not used for tracking users across apps or websites.

Third-Party Services

Navine Homework Helper may utilize third-party technologies necessary for app functionality, hosting, security, or educational assistance features. Any such services are used solely to provide and improve the app experience and are subject to their own privacy practices.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the app and strive to maintain appropriate security safeguards against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Children’s Privacy

Navine Homework Helper is intended for students and learners of various ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary to provide the educational services of the app. Parents or guardians who have concerns may contact the developer.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be made available on this page. Continued use of Navine Homework Helper after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the platform where Navine Homework Helper is distributed.
