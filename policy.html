Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 20, 2025

Overview

Simple New Tab Override is a lightweight browser extension that allows you to customize the page shown when you open a new tab. Your privacy is important, and this extension has been designed to respect it completely.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing activity, or usage information.

All settings (such as your chosen new tab URL) are stored locally in your browser using the built-in extension storage system. This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

The extension requests only the minimal permissions required to function:

  • newTabOverride – to replace the browser’s default new tab page.
  • storage – to save your chosen custom URL locally.

No other permissions (such as web access, cookies, or network requests) are used.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published in the extension listing and on this page. You are encouraged to review this page periodically.
