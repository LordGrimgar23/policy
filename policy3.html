Privacy Policy

Navine Volume Booster

This Privacy Policy explains how Navine Volume Booster handles user data. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how this application works.

Overview

Navine Volume Booster is a simple utility designed solely to increase or amplify audio volume on your device or within supported applications. It performs no other functions beyond volume enhancement.

Data Collection

Navine Volume Booster does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing activity is tracked
  • No audio content is recorded or transmitted
  • No analytics or telemetry are used

Permissions

Any permissions requested by Navine Volume Booster are used exclusively to enable its core functionality—boosting audio volume. These permissions are not used for tracking, monitoring, or data collection.

Third-Party Services

Navine Volume Booster does not integrate with or rely on any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or stored, there is no user data at risk. All processing related to volume enhancement occurs locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Navine Volume Booster is safe for use by users of all ages. As no data collection occurs, no personal information from children or adults is gathered.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be made available on this page. Continued use of Navine Volume Booster after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the platform where Navine Volume Booster is distributed.
