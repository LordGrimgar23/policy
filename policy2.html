Crunchyroll Commenter — Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Crunchyroll Commenter is a Chrome extension that allows users to rate, comment, and review videos on Crunchyroll (and optionally other sites). We take your privacy seriously and do not collect personal data without your consent.

2. Information We Store

The extension stores your comments, ratings, and reviews using your browser’s local or synced storage (chrome.storage.local / chrome.storage.sync). These are only accessible to you unless you opt-in to share reviews globally through a public API.

3. Optional Global Mode

If you enable the Global Comments feature, your reviews will be sent to a shared server to make them visible to all users of the extension. No account, email, or identifying information is required or transmitted.

4. Data Security

We use secure HTTPS connections for any data transmitted in global mode. Locally stored data remains on your browser and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your Chrome storage.

5. Third Parties

Crunchyroll Commenter is not affiliated with Crunchyroll, LLC. This extension only interacts with Crunchyroll pages through your browser and does not access your Crunchyroll account or credentials.

6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page, and the “last updated” date will be adjusted accordingly.

7. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding your data, please reach out via the project’s GitHub page or email: support@crcommenter.dev.
